📖 Game Guide

YOUR ROLE

HOW TO PLAY

Objective: Good wins by succeeding 3 quests. Evil wins by failing 3 quests or correctly assassinating Merlin.

Night Phase: All players secretly see their roles. Special roles gain knowledge of others.

Day Phase: The Leader proposes a team for the quest. All players vote Approve or Reject. If rejected 5 times, Evil wins automatically.

Quest Phase: Team members secretly vote Success or Fail. Even 1 Fail card fails the quest (2 required in some rounds).

Assassination: If Good wins 3 quests, Evil's Assassin gets one chance to identify and kill Merlin. Success = Evil wins.

Team Sizes: Follow the leader board displayed in-game for each quest's required team size.
