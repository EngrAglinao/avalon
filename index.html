📖 Game Guide
How to Play
⚔️ The Leader nominates a team for each Quest. The group votes to Approve or Reject it.
⚔️ 5 consecutive Rejections in one round = automatic Evil victory.
✨ Good wins 3 Quests, then the Assassin gets one chance to identify Merlin.
💀 Evil wins 3 Quests, OR the Assassin correctly names Merlin.
🗳️ Quest votes: Good players must play Success. Evil can play Success or Fail (secretly).
👥 Quest 4 with 7+ players requires 2 Fail cards to fail.
